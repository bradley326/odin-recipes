Lasagna

Lasagna

Description

This is a classic Italian-American comfort food. It consists of layers of wide, thin pasta, a seasoned ricotta cheese mixture, and meat sauce stacked on top of one another. The dish is topped with more meat sauce and a generous coating of mozarella cheese.

Ingredients:

  • lasagna pasta (15 noodles)
  • Ground beef (2 pounds)
  • marinara pasta sauce
  • ricotta cheese (16 ounces)
  • Italian seasoning (oregano, parsley, basil)
  • egg (1)
  • mozarella cheese (1 pound)

Steps:

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  2. While noodles are boiling, fry ground beef until done. Drain grease.
  3. Mix ground beef with marinara sauce.
  4. Add the egg to the ricotta cheese and mix well.
  5. In a square pan, layer noodles, meat sauce mixture, and ricotta cheese mixture in that order.
  6. Layer until reaching the top of the pan.
  7. Add a final layer of meat sauce and top with mozarella cheese.
  8. Cook in the oven at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
